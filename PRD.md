# Product Requirements Document: AIfy Interiors

## Overview
AIfy Interiors is a comprehensive AI-powered interior design platform serving consumers, professionals, and the Indian market with tailored solutions for each segment.

## Vision
Make professional interior design accessible globally through AI-powered visualization and design recommendations, with specialized features for different markets and user types.

## Product Tiers

### Tier 1: AIfy Interiors (Consumer)
**Target Audience:**
- Homeowners planning renovations
- Renters looking to optimize their space
- Interior design enthusiasts
- Real estate agents staging properties
- DIY home decorators

### Tier 2: Interiors AI India
**Target Audience:**
- Indian homeowners
- Real estate developers in India
- Small interior design firms
- Architects and builders in India
- Furniture retailers in India

### Tier 3: Interiors AI Pro (Professional)
**Target Audience:**
- Professional interior designers
- Architecture firms
- Commercial design studios
- Hospitality design specialists
- Corporate office designers
- Retail space planners

## Core Features

### Tier 1: AIfy Interiors (Consumer Features)

#### 1. AI Room Visualization
- Upload photos of existing rooms
- AI-powered redesign with different styles
- Furniture and decor recommendations
- Color scheme suggestions
- Before/after visualizations

#### 2. Style Library
- Multiple design styles (Modern, Scandinavian, Industrial, Bohemian, etc.)
- Room-specific designs (bedroom, living room, kitchen, bathroom)
- Seasonal and trending styles
- Budget-friendly options

#### 3. Virtual Staging
- Empty room staging for real estate
- 3D rendering capabilities
- Multiple design variations
- AR visualization of products in space

#### 4. Shopping Integration
- Direct links to recommended furniture and decor
- Price comparison across retailers
- Budget-friendly alternatives
- Affiliate partnerships

### Tier 2: Interiors AI India (India-Specific Features)

#### 1. Localized Design
- Indian home layout optimization
- Regional style preferences (Traditional, Contemporary, Fusion)
- Vastu-compliant designs
- Space-saving solutions for compact homes
- Climate-appropriate recommendations
- Multi-generational living spaces
- Prayer room and pooja mandir designs

#### 2. Indian Market Integration
- Integration with Indian furniture marketplaces (Pepperfry, Urban Ladder, IKEA India)
- Local vendor recommendations
- Budget estimation in INR
- Material availability in local markets
- Installation service provider network

#### 3. Cultural Elements
- Traditional Indian motifs and patterns
- Festival and occasion-specific decor
- Indian kitchen and dining layouts
- Budget planning with phased implementation
- DIY vs professional recommendations

#### 4. Multi-language Support
- Interface in Hindi, English, and 10+ regional languages
- Local customer support
- Payment integration (Razorpay, Paytm, UPI)

### Tier 3: Interiors AI Pro (Professional Features - All Consumer Features Plus)

#### 1. Professional 3D Rendering
- Photorealistic 3D renders
- Multiple lighting scenarios
- Material and texture library
- Custom furniture modeling
- Virtual walkthroughs
- VR/AR visualization

#### 2. Client Presentation Tools
- Interactive design presentations
- Mood board creation
- Multiple design concept comparison
- Real-time client collaboration
- Professional portfolio showcase

#### 3. Project Management
- Client project tracking
- Budget and timeline management
- Vendor and contractor coordination
- Material procurement tracking
- Installation scheduling
- Invoice and billing system

#### 4. Commercial Design Tools
- Space planning optimization
- Building code compliance checking
- Accessibility standards (ADA/Universal Design compliance)
- Sustainability scoring
- Cost estimation and bidding
- Commercial furniture catalogs

## Technical Stack
- Frontend: React + TypeScript
- Backend: Node.js + Express
- Database: PostgreSQL with geospatial extensions
- AI: Stable Diffusion for image generation, GPT-4 for recommendations
- 3D: Three.js, Unreal Engine (Pro tier)
- VR/AR: WebXR, Unity integration (Pro tier)
- Storage: Cloud storage for uploads and generated images
- Payments: Stripe (Global), Razorpay/Paytm (India)
- Localization: i18n framework, 15+ languages
